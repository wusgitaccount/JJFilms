JJ Farry - 2025... 2024... 2023... 2022... 2021... Catharsis (2021) ...

My first foray into ‘serious’ filmmaking, Catharsis is a culmination of pent up teenage angst and edge. Lacking any subtlety and nuance, this film depicts a hyper-dramatised response to bullying, something that I felt very passionate about as a victim to bullying for several years of my schooling. Created with almost no external support, the film feels very rough around the edges, but still occupies a soft spot in my heart for showing me that filmmaking was more than just a hobby.
