Riding off the latent teenage angst explored in Catharsis, this film aims to present a more mature examination of a damaged character, with debatable success. Exploring darker themes such as impulsiveness, selfishness and detachment, the un-named main character played by Dingkai Lu was my interpretation of modern evil. This film still suffers from the natural lack of subtlety that only an eighteen-year-old could possess, meaning it feels very on the nose and clumsy. So much was learnt from the film's creation however, and it marked a big stepping point in my development as a filmmaker.
