Created for an assignment during my first year of uni, Any Other Day marks my first attempt to explore different styles of filmmaking and storytelling. Featuring my Dad John Farry Sr, this film portrays his daily pre-work routine. Opening with a staple alarm clock shot, we watch as he performs mundane tasks such as making his bed and putting his work uniform on. There is a melancholie within his acting however that I personally feel adds a tender touch to the piece. The visuals are also accompanied by a monologue written and performed by my Dad, that outlines his outlook on his work. His words are admittedly made to feel a lot more melodramatic, as his feelings in regards to work are a lot more optimistic and hopeful, but we both agreed to exaggerate everything a bit for the film.
